# CreatorFunds ‚Äî "Here's to the Creators"

## Overview
A bold, animated storytelling landing page for CreatorFunds.org ‚Äî an influencer-brand matching agency that creates a fund to help smaller creators get deals while matching larger creators with premium brands.

Inspired by Apple's legendary "Think Different" campaign, this page tells the story of creators as the new change-makers.

### 2. Creator Fund (`creator-fund.png`)
**Use for**: Section 5 - The Fund Concept

```
A small seedling or plant growing from coins/currency, bathed in golden morning light.
Style: Warm, optimistic, growth metaphor.

Mood: Investment that grows, nurturing potential.
```

**Alternative prompt (more conceptual):**

### 3. Creator Collective / Tapestry (`creator-collective.png`)
**Use for**: Section 8 - The Collective

```
A beautiful woven tapestry visualization ‚Äî many threads of different colors 
(coral, gold, purple, mint, blue, pink) coming together to form a cohesive pattern.
Each thread represents a different creator voice.
Style: Abstract but warm, textile-inspired digital art.
Colors: Full brand palette woven together harmoniously.
Mood: Unity in diversity, collective strength, collaborative beauty.
Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square)
Details: Some threads should be thinner (smaller creators), some thicker (bigger creators),
```

**Alternative prompt (more human):**
```
An aerial view of diverse people standing in a circle, holding hands or linking arms,

The shadows overlap and blend where people connect, creating a kaleidoscope effect.
Style: Modern photography meets digital art, soft lighting.
Figures are abstract/silhouetted, focus on the connection and color interplay.
```

**Alternative prompt (abstract):**
```
A constellation of glowing orbs of varying sizes, all connected by delicate 
luminous threads. The orbs pulse gently, and the connections brighten when 
multiple orbs are linked. Smaller orbs are just as connected as larger ones.
Style: 3D render, dark background with vibrant gradient colors.
```
